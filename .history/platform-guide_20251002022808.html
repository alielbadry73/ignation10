Getting Started

1
Account Registration

Create your account by providing your email, name, and choosing a secure password. Your account will be activated after email verification.

2
Profile Setup

Complete your profile by adding your academic level, subjects of interest, and learning preferences to get personalized recommendations.

3
Course Enrollment

Browse available courses, read descriptions, and enroll in courses that match your learning goals. Payment is processed securely through our platform.

Pro Tip

Start with one course to get familiar with the platform before enrolling in multiple courses. This helps you establish a consistent learning routine.

Learning Tools

Todo Lists

Organize your study tasks with our built-in todo list system:

  • Create multiple lists for different subjects or courses
  • Set priorities (High, Medium, Low) for each task
  • Mark tasks as completed to track your progress
  • Add due dates and reminders for important assignments
Study Materials

Access a variety of study materials:

  • Lecture Notes: Comprehensive notes accompanying video lectures
  • Practice Problems: Extensive problem sets with solutions
  • Reference Materials: Textbooks, formulas, and quick reference guides
  • Study Guides: Subject-specific study guides and revision materials
Study Tip

Use the todo lists to break down large assignments into smaller, manageable tasks. This makes studying more organized and less overwhelming.

Assessments & Exams

Types of Assessments

Regular assignments to reinforce learning with immediate feedback and detailed explanations.

Practice Quizzes

Short quizzes to test understanding of specific topics with instant results.

Formal Exams

Comprehensive exams with time limits and detailed performance analytics.

Assessment Guidelines

Before Taking Assessments:

  • Review all relevant course materials
  • Complete practice problems and quizzes
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Find a quiet environment free from distractions

During Assessments:

  • Read all instructions carefully
  • Manage your time effectively
  • Answer all questions to the best of your ability
  • Save your work regularly if the platform allows
Important

Academic integrity is essential. All assessments must be completed independently without external help or resources unless specifically permitted.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions
Video Playback Issues

Problem: Videos not loading or playing properly

Solutions:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try using a different browser
  • Disable browser extensions temporarily
  • Update your browser to the latest version
Login Problems

Problem: Unable to log into your account

Solutions:

  • Verify your email and password are correct
  • Check if Caps Lock is enabled
  • Try resetting your password
  • Clear browser data and try again
  • Contact support if the problem persists
Assignment Submission Issues

Problem: Cannot submit assignments or quizzes

Solutions:

  • Check if you're logged into your account
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Try refreshing the page
  • Check if the assignment deadline has passed
  • Contact your instructor if technical issues persist
Technical Support

If you continue to experience issues, please contact our technical support team with detailed information about the problem, including your browser type, operating system, and any error messages you've received.
